Who We Are

We are a small tricking group located in Omaha, Nebraska. Our group consists of trickers, free runners, gymnasts and breakdancers.

What is Tricking?

Tricking is a mix of martial arts and gymnastics; flips, twists and kicks are the weapons of choice. Explosive movements are combined into a spectacular form of art and expression.

Where We Trick?

Wherever we want! The beauty of tricking is that it can be done just about anywhere; inside, outside, on grass, on concrete. The world is your sandbox.
